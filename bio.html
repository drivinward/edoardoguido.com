Who is he?

Born 1993 in Monza, an idyllic city close to Milan, he has studied English (C1 Trinity College London), French (B2 DELF) and German (B1 ZDJ) in High School, where he graduated with a thesis on the importance of Humanities (yes, he still thinks they are important, and always will).

Because of his curiosity towards technology and computers since the age of four (when his father taught him to press the Return key), he then decided to choose the path of design.

After gaining experience by himself (he got featured in the Interaction Gallery on BÄ“hance at the age of 16), he studied Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano, where he could further improve his creative abilities.

The main outcome of such studies is the ability to design and control complex processes through a variety of technical and creative tools.
