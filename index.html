Who am I, anyways?

Some people call me Edo, some others Edoguido. Fact is, I was Born in Monza, Lombardy, in 1993. During my high school studies I discovered UI and UX Design, therefore I decided to attend the Communication Design course in Politecnico di Milano, where I already got a Bachelor Degree and am currently finishing my Master of Arts.

I find particular pleasure in designing and coding experiences for users, specially by thinking with motion, which I consider an important and meaningful tool.

I also am a deep geek: messing around is my specialty, also with Python (I wrote a simple and hopefully useful tool, too) and Raspberry Pi.

Last but not least: I am a productivity and shortcut maniac.

I'm a communication designer focused on UX
who also loves coding, python and dataviz.
