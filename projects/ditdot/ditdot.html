ditdot —
Final university project

ditdot is a fictitious brand that was created for Laboratorio di Sintesi (Synthesis Lab), that is the last phase of a three-year course in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano. Teachers Francesco Guida, Andrea Braccaloni, Pietro Buffa Di Castelalto gave students the task of rebranding a common-use object (in this case a hammer) and designing a "communicative machine", which is an interactive artifact that users can interact with and can get to know the brand and its core values. As a result, ditdot heavily relies on sensory branding by starting from the sound that the hammer makes when using it, and transposing it into colour.
