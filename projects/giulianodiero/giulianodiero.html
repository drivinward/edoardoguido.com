Giuliano Diero.

Client: Giuliano Diero

Project start date: October 2016


Giuliano Diero is an Italian furniture manufacturer.
The company has many clients around the world, especially in the Middle-East (Saudi Arabia and Azerbaijan just to mention a few) and Russia, providing high-quality and luxury furniture with a vintage-looking style.


Concept.


An interview with the client revealed the way in which customers always place orders for a product: they have some references to show, and then decide to customize the details of it, also creating compositions between sets of furniture.

This is what has been done with the logo and logotype: starting from a reference typeface, the two capital letters have been customized to be recognizable and complementary.
