A Lego™ Story —
"Ognuno è fabbro della propria fortuna"

The Computer Graphics course in Politecnico di Milano (lead by teachers Marisa Bertolo, requires students to pick a random saying and build a story on it.
The process is not immediate though, since the students have to design all the ambients, the characters, and a small script in order to give birth to the full project.
The saying that was picked up is "Ognuno è fabbro della propria fortuna", meaning that "everyone forges ('is the blacksmith of') his/her own fortune".

Project By Edoardo Guido, Elvira Lo Torto, Luca Marsano, Annunziato Mazzaferro.
